Crystals Collector


How to play

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game
  • You will be adding points to the toal score by clicking a crystal (each crystal has a different value)
  • You win when your total score matches to the random number
  • You lose when your total score exceeds the random number
  • Each time when the game starts, the value of each crystal changes
